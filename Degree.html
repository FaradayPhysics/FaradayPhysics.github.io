Relatively Interesting: Degree Level Physics

The materials collected here are intended to provide a thorough grounding in the fundamentals of modern physics, at an approximately undergraduate level. The materials here are structured much like they would be in a university degree: divided into discrete courses for different topics. Each course consists of a set of notes, problems, and solutions. The courses are listed in the order in which they are designed to be studied; however, they are intended to be as modular as possible so that one can dip in and out as they desire.
